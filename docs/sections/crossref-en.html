IRI: https://city-artificial-intelligence.github.io/diso/ontology#Cluster

A subdomain grouping (cluster) within an ontology network. Its void:subset members are member ontologies, or — for a grouping cluster — further sub-clusters.

IRI: https://city-artificial-intelligence.github.io/diso/ontology#OntologyNetwork

A curated network (collection) of ontologies, described as a VoID dataset whose void:subset members are its clusters. DISO itself is the sole instance.

SPDX licence expressiondp back to ToC or Data Property ToC

IRI: https://city-artificial-intelligence.github.io/diso/ontology#spdxLicenseExpression

The licence of a dataset expressed as an SPDX licence expression, recorded verbatim. Captures compound expressions (e.g. 'MIT AND CC-BY-4.0') and the value 'NOASSERTION' where no licence could be established.
