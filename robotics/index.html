Robotics ontologies

The DISO robotics cluster of ontologies emerged largely due to our discovery of the CORA ontology (a network of ontologies for IEEE robotics standards).

The OntoSecRPA ontology, in the DISO information-security cluster, also addresses the robotics domain (Robotics Process Automation). OntoSecRPA is an example of an ontology that fits well in more than one DISO cluster. It sits in the information-security cluster simply because that is where it was first assigned.

So the DISO robotics cluster should be thought of as containing two ontologies, not one: CORA and OntoSecRPA.
