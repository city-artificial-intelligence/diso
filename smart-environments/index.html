Smart Environments

The DISO contains a number of ontologies that model environments of different scale and scope. Most of these use the word smart in relation to themselves.

From largest to smallest scale/scope, the different categories of environments we have encountered are: * cities * buildings * homes

DISO has invented the phrase smart environments to act as a general name for this cluster of environment-related ontologies.

Note that the notion of smart environments overlaps strongly with the notion of ambient intelligence, and hence with context-awareness, and thus situation-awareness.

The notion of smart environments also overlaps strongly with the notion of digital twins, and hence with the UK's National Digital Twin Programme.
